     what is the solar maximum with regard to earths distance from the sun - what date will we hit maximum

The Solar Maximum occurs when the Earth is at its closest point from the Sun, usually around January 3. At its closest point, the Earth is about 91.4 million miles from the Sun. This is the point when the Sun's energy is most intense, and solar flares are at their most frequent. So, we will hit the Solar Maximum on January 3.
