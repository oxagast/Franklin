Commodore PET (Personal Electronic Transactor) was a series of home and small-business computers produced by Commodore Business Machines (CBM) from 1977 to 1990. They were the first mass-produced personal computers, and the first computers sold to the general public. The PET was the first of a series of computers from Commodore, including the Commodore VIC-20, Commodore 64, and Commodore 128.
