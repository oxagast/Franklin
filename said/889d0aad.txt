At what US stadiums did the rock band The Guess Who play
-------- snip --------


The Guess Who played at venues across the United States, including Madison Square Garden in New York City, the Universal Amphitheatre in Los Angeles, the Cow Palace in San Francisco, the Metrodome in Minneapolis, the Joe Louis Arena in Detroit, the Capital Centre in Landover, Maryland, and the Red Rocks Amphitheater in Denver.
