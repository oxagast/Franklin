I am that I am is a phrase used in the Bible to refer to God's name. The phrase is considered to be a declaration of God's existence and self-sufficiency, affirming that God is the only one who can define himself. It is also interpreted as an affirmation of God's power and authority, and as an expression of his unchangeable nature.
