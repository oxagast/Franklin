Sumatra is the sixth largest island in the world and part of the Indonesian archipelago. It is located in western Indonesia, between the Indian and Pacific oceans. It is home to some of the world’s most diverse wildlife, and its tropical rainforests are among the world’s oldest and most beautiful. Sumatra is also home to several active volcanoes and is known for its rich culture and heritage.
