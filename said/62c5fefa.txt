Benjamin Franklin is widely considered to be one of the Founding Fathers of the United States and is credited with helping to draft the Declaration of Independence and the U.S. Constitution, as well as negotiating the 1783 Treaty of Paris that ended the American Revolutionary War. He is also renowned for his many inventions, including the lightning rod, bifocals, and the Franklin stove.
