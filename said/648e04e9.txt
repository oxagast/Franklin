According to nutrition data, pasta and beans have a higher amount of protein and fiber compared to rice and beans. They also have a similar amount of carbohydrates and calories, but pasta and beans have more vitamins and minerals. However, rice and beans have a lower glycemic index, making it a better option for those with diabetes or blood sugar concerns. Ultimately, both are nutritious options for a balanced meal.
