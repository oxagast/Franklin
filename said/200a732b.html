     What is pex plumbing

PEX plumbing is a type of plastic piping that is used in a variety of residential and commercial applications. It is often used in place of copper and other metal piping due to its resistance to corrosion, flexibility, and cost. PEX pipes are connected using fittings and clamps, making installation easier and faster than traditional methods.
