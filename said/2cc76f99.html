The dealer then discards one card, face-up, onto the crib. The non-dealer then plays the first card. Play alternates between the two players and they must respond with a card of the same suit if they can. When neither player can follow suit, the player with the highest card in that suit wins the trick. After all cards have been played, the dealer counts the points in the hands and in the crib. The winner of the hand is the player with the most points.
