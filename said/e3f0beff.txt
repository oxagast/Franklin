No Man of God is a 2021 American biographical drama film directed by Mark A. Lewis and written by Mark A. Lewis and Dave Alan Johnson. The film stars Luke Kleintank, Roberta Maxwell and Cary Elwes and tells the story of Father John Geoghan, an Irish-American Catholic priest who was convicted of molesting over 130 children. The film follows Father Geoghans trial, conviction and eventual death in prison and explores the systemic issues behind the Catholic Churchs handling of the scandal.
