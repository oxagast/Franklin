A New World Order is a term used to describe a new international order, typically one in which the superpowers of the world cooperate and collaborate to achieve global peace and stability. It is based on the idea that international law and norms should be followed and respected by all nations, regardless of their political or economic systems. This concept has been used to describe various political and economic theories, including those of communism, socialism, and neoliberalism.
