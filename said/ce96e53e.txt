Symptoms of acute autism can vary from person to person and can include:

• Social isolation and difficulty forming relationships with peers
• Avoidance of eye contact and physical contact
• Difficulty with verbal and nonverbal communication
• Repetitive behaviors and restricted interests
• Difficulty adjusting to changes in routine
• Unusual responses to sensory experiences
• Difficulty understanding and responding to emotions
• Poor motor skills
• Poor problem-solving skills
