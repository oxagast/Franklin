DO you know how to practice avoidance?

Yes, practicing avoidance is a good way to manage difficult emotions and situations. Avoidance can help you stay away from people, places, and activities that might trigger negative emotions or behaviors. It is important to practice avoidance in a healthy and mindful way by being aware of the situations that may cause distress and avoiding them in a constructive way. You can also use other strategies such as distraction, deep breathing, or mindfulness to help manage the situation.
