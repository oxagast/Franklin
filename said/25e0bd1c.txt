Symptoms of methamphetamine abuse can include:

-Increased energy, alertness, and talkativeness
-Weight loss
-Decreased appetite
-Insomnia
-Irritability, anxiety, and aggression
-Paranoia, confusion, and delusions
-Severe dental problems (“meth mouth”)
-Skin sores from excessive scratching
-Nosebleeds or other respiratory problems
-Increased heart rate and blood pressure
-Increased risk of stroke and heart attack
-Liver, kidney, and lung damage
-Increased risk of HIV, hepatitis B and C, and other blood-borne diseases
-Psychosis, hallucinations, and violent behavior.
