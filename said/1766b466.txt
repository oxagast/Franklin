Ethics is a set of moral principles or values that guide how people behave in different situations. Professional ethics is a specific set of moral principles or values that guide how people behave in the workplace. Professional ethics typically includes principles such as honesty, objectivity, respect, and fairness. It also includes guidelines on how to treat others, handle confidential information, and how to handle conflicts of interest.
