Cultural Marxism is a form of Marxism that emphasizes the importance of culture in the development of economic and political ideologies and theories. It is concerned with the power of culture in shaping human behavior and creating social structures and hierarchies. It sees culture as an expression of a society's existing power relations and sees culture as a tool to create and maintain oppressive power relations.
