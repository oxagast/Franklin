Scalped is a crime drama comic book series written by Jason Aaron and illustrated by R.M Guera. It is set in the fictional Prairie Rose Indian Reservation in South Dakota and is set in the present day. It follows the lives of a large cast of characters, including Dashiell Bad Horse, a former criminal who has returned to the reservation to investigate corruption. The series deals with themes of poverty, crime, and the exploitation of Native Americans.
