Mallow (Malva) is a genus of flowering plants in the family Malvaceae, which includes over 200 species of annual, biennial, and perennial herbs native to temperate, subtropical, and tropical regions throughout the world. Many species are edible and have multiple uses, including in traditional medicine. In some countries, mallow is used as a salad green.
