<---- snip ---->
1. Press the Windows key and type in “User Accounts”.
2. Select the “Change your account type” option.
3. Select the account you want to change and click on “Change the account type”.
4. Select “Administrator” from the list of account types.
5. Click “Change Account Type” to save the changes.
