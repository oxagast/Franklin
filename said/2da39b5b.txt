Thai-Japanese-Mexican-French Fusion Tacos:

Ingredients: 
-Taco shells
-1 lb ground chicken
-1 can black beans
-1/2 cup cooked white rice
-1/2 cup shredded cabbage
-1/2 cup diced red onion
-1/4 cup diced jalapeno
-1/4 cup diced Thai basil
-1/4 cup diced cilantro
-1/4 cup diced scallions
-1/4 cup diced red bell pepper
-1/4 cup diced mango
-1/4 cup diced avocado
-1/4 cup diced pineapple
-1/4 cup diced French shallots
-1/4 cup diced French chives
-1/4 cup diced French garlic
-1/4 cup diced French parsley
-1/4 cup diced French thyme
-1/4 cup diced French tarragon
-1/4 cup diced French chervil
-1 teaspoon garlic powder
-1 teaspoon onion powder
-1 teaspoon smoked paprika
-1 teaspoon cumin
-1 teaspoon coriander
-1 teaspoon oregano
-1/2 teaspoon chili powder
-1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
-1/4 teaspoon salt
-1/4 teaspoon black pepper
-1/2 cup Japanese mayonnaise
-1/4 cup Mexican crema
-1/4 cup Thai sweet chili sauce

Instructions: 
1. Preheat oven to 375°F.

2. Place taco shells on a baking sheet and bake for 8-10 minutes, until lightly browned and crisp.

3. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the ground chicken until browned and cooked through.

4. Add the black beans, white rice, cabbage, red onion, jalapeno, Thai basil, cilantro, scallions, red bell pepper, mango, avocado, pineapple, French shallots, French chives, French garlic, French parsley, French thyme, French tarragon, and French chervil to the skillet.

5. Stir in the garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, cumin, coriander, oregano, chili powder, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper.

6. Cook, stirring often, until everything is heated through and the vegetables are tender.

7. To assemble the tacos, fill each taco shell with the chicken and vegetable mixture.

8. Drizzle with Japanese mayonnaise, Mexican crema, and Thai sweet chili sauce.

9. Serve and enjoy!
