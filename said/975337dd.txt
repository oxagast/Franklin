It is the story of a young girl named Riley, who is uprooted from her Midwest life when her father starts a new job in San Francisco. Rileys emotions—Joy, Fear, Anger, Disgust, and Sadness—live in Headquarters, the control center inside Rileys mind, where they help advise her through everyday life. As Riley and her emotions struggle to adjust to the big changes in her life, they must work together to help her build a new life in her new home.
