To Whom It May Concern,

I doth humbly beg to inform you that I am resigning from my position at this fine establishment. It hath been a great pleasure to serve here for so many years and I must express my sincerest gratitude for the opportunity to ply my trade.

I doth wish you and your business all the best in the future. My last day of service will be on the morrow, when I shall have completed my present duties.

Once again, I thank you for the privilege of working here. I remain

Your humble servant, 

Elizabeth
