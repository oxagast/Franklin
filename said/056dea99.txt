1. Transceiver: A transceiver is the main component of an SSB HF radio, as it is responsible for transmitting and receiving signals.

2. Antenna: An antenna is needed to receive and transmit radio waves.

3. Power Supply: A power supply is needed to provide the necessary power to the radio.

4. Antenna Tuner: An antenna tuner is needed to match the antenna to the radio's output.

5. Microphone: A microphone is needed to transmit voice signals.

6. Headphones: Headphones are needed to hear the incoming signals.
