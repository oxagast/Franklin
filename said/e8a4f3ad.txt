Dude, Wheres My Car? is a 2000 American comedy film about two young men who wake up after a night of partying and cant remember where they parked their car. Jesse and Chester, two dim-witted stoners, wake up one morning from a night of partying and cannot remember where they parked their car. Their quest to locate their car blows their minds as they encounter hot alien chicks, a trashed house, a dangerous biker gang, and a mysterious cult of vegetarians.
