Hacking is a skill set not a malicious procedure
-------- snip --------


Hacking is a broad term that can refer to many different activities. It is often used to refer to activities that involve modifying or exploiting computer systems to gain unauthorized access to data or resources. However, it can also refer to activities such as finding creative solutions to complex problems or using technology to improve existing systems. Hacking is a skill set, not a malicious procedure, and many hackers use their skills to improve security, create new software, and uncover vulnerabilities in existing systems.
