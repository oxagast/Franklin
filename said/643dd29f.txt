SADS stands for Sudden Arrhythmia Death Syndromes and is a group of conditions that cause sudden cardiac arrest. These conditions include Long QT Syndrome, Brugada Syndrome, and Catecholaminergic Polymorphic Ventricular Tachycardia, among others. SADS can be fatal and can occur with no warning signs or symptoms in seemingly healthy people.
