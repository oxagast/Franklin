DO stands for Doctor of Osteopathic Medicine (also known as Doctor of Osteopathy). It is a professional doctoral degree for physicians and surgeons offered by medical schools in the United States. Osteopathic medicine is a holistic approach to medicine that emphasizes preventive care, the bodys natural tendency toward self-healing, and the integration of all aspects of patient care, including the mind, body, and spirit.
