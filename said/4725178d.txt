Vallabhbhai Jhaverbhai Patel, popularly known as Sardar Patel, was an Indian barrister and statesman, one of the leaders of the Indian National Congress and one of the founding fathers of the Republic of India. He was the first Deputy Prime Minister of India. He was instrumental in the integration of 562 princely states into the Union of India and was responsible for creating the Indian Administrative Service (IAS). He is also known as the "Iron Man of India" for his strong will and determination to unify the country.
