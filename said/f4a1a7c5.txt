1. To program the ESN slots on the OKI 900, press the * and # keys simultaneously to enter the programming mode.

2. Enter the ESN (electronic serial number) in the slot.

3. Press the # key to enter the next slot.

4. Enter the NAM (name) in the slot.

5. Press the # key to save the programmed information.

6. Press the * key to exit programming mode.
