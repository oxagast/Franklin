The BBL is a multi-jurisdictional initiative to create a regional economic development alliance and to coordinate economic development efforts throughout the region. The four counties are served by the Greater Lehigh Valley Chamber of Commerce, which serves as the regional coordinating organization for the BBL. The BBL works to identify and support regional economic development projects and programs that create jobs, spur investment, and foster collaboration.
