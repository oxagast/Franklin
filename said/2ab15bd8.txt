The most important e-government startup in India is Paytm. Founded in 2010, Paytm is an e-commerce payment system and digital wallet company based in India. It provides a platform for online payments, mobile recharges, bill payments, and financial services such as mutual funds and insurance. It is also the largest digital payment platform in India and has been used by millions of users for digital transactions.
