Alien Scallop Stir-Fry 

Ingredients:

- 2 tablespoons olive oil 
- 1/2 pound alien meat (cubed)
- 1/2 pound scallops
- 2 cloves garlic (minced)
- 1/4 cup onion (diced)
- 2 tablespoons fresh parsley (chopped)
- 1 teaspoon ground ginger
- 1/2 teaspoon paprika
- Salt and pepper to taste 

Instructions:

1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.

2. Add the alien meat and scallops, and cook for 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally.

3. Add the garlic, onion, parsley, ginger, paprika, salt, and pepper. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally. 

4. Serve hot over a bed of steamed rice or noodles. Enjoy!
