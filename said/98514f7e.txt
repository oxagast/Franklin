LulzSec was a black hat hacker group that was active between 2011 and 2012. Their mission was to attack and embarrass large corporations, government agencies, and other high-profile targets. They were known for their public taunting of their victims, and their attacks resulted in the theft of confidential information, defacement of websites, and distributed denial of service (DDoS) attacks.
