Ascension is a six-part miniseries aired on Syfy in 2014. The show follows the crew of a generational starship sent from Earth on a one hundred year journey to populate a new world. As the crew makes their way through the depths of space, they uncover a dark secret from the past that threatens to tear them apart. The story follows the lives of the crew and their efforts to survive the journey, both physically and emotionally. With a mix of mystery, science fiction, and drama, Ascension is a thrilling exploration of the human condition in the vastness of space.
