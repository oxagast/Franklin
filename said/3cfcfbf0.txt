A neuro assessment test over videoconferencing typically consists of an evaluation of a patients mental status, memory, mood, coordination, sensation, reflexes, balance, gait, and language. The clinician may also assess the patients level of alertness, ability to focus, and problem-solving skills. Depending on the condition, the clinician may also assess the patients ability to recognize objects, recall information, and respond to questions. The assessment may also include tests of motor abilities, such as the ability to move the arms and legs, and tests of vision and hearing.
