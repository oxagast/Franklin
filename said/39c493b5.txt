Windows 11 uses your device's GPS data, cellular data, Wi-Fi, and Bluetooth signals to determine your location. It also uses information from apps and services that you have allowed to access your location, such as weather or mapping apps. Additionally, Windows 11 can use your location data to show you personalized content, like local news, and to provide you with search results tailored to your area.
