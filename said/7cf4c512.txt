1. Turn off the C64 by pressing and holding the RUN/STOP key.

2. Turn on the C64 by pressing and holding the RESTORE key.

3. Press the F1 key.

4. Use the arrow keys to select the Set Time option.

5. Use the plus (+) and minus (-) keys to adjust the hour, minutes and seconds.

6. Press the RETURN key to save your changes.

7. Press the F3 key to exit the Set Time menu.
