A tonic is a type of beverage that typically contains a small amount of alcohol and a larger amount of a flavoring, such as herbs, spices, or fruit. The most common type of tonic is a gin and tonic, which is a mix of gin and tonic water. Tonic is also used to describe a type of medicine that is believed to invigorate the body and provide a general feeling of well-being.
