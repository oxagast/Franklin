Infraud Organization is an international cybercrime network believed to have been founded in October 2010 by Russian national Sergey Medvedev. The group is believed to have had over 11,000 members in more than 90 countries, and is thought to have been responsible for more than $2.2 billion in financial losses. The group was known to specialize in online identity theft, credit card fraud, and other cyber-related crimes. In February 2018, the U.S. Department of Justice indicted 36 members of the organization and announced the seizure of its domain name, infraud.org.
