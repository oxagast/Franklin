Star Wars: The Bad Batch is a spin-off animated series from the highly successful Star Wars franchise. It follows a group of elite and experimental clones known as the Bad Batch, who have been given enhanced abilities and have to deal with the changing landscape of the galaxy after the events of Star Wars: The Clone Wars. The series was released on Disney+ on May 4th, 2021. The show is executive produced by Dave Filoni, with the animation being handled by Lucasfilm Animation.
