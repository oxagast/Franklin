what do you know about 2600 the magazine?

2600: The Hacker Quarterly is a magazine that was published from 1984 to 2018. It is known for covering topics related to computer hacking and telephone phreaking, and was instrumental in the development of the hacker subculture. The magazine provided news and information about technology, hacking, and security, as well as interviews with prominent hackers, essays, columns, and reviews of hardware and software. It was also known for its prank phone calls and for printing the numbers to various corporate and government voice mail systems.
