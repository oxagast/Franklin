Seaweed Soup with Brewer's Yeast Dumplings

Ingredients:

-2 tablespoons olive oil

-2 cloves garlic, minced

-1 onion, diced

-1 leek, sliced

-4 cups vegetable broth

-1 cup dried seaweed, such as dulse or kelp

-1 teaspoon dried thyme

-1 teaspoon dried oregano

-1 teaspoon sea salt

-1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

-1/4 cup brewer's yeast

-3 tablespoons all-purpose flour

-2 tablespoons non-dairy butter

-1/2 cup non-dairy milk

-3 tablespoons nutritional yeast

-1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Instructions:

1. Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the garlic, onion, and leek and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.

2. Add the vegetable broth, seaweed, thyme, oregano, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 15 minutes.

3. In a small bowl, whisk together the brewer's yeast, flour, butter, and non-dairy milk until a thick batter forms.

4. Drop spoonfuls of the batter into the soup and simmer for 10 minutes.

5. Add the nutritional yeast and parsley and stir to combine. Simmer for 5 more minutes.

6. Serve hot. Enjoy!
