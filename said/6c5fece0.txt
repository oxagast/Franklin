Camp Lejeune is a United States Marine Corps base located in Jacksonville, North Carolina. Established in 1941, it is one of the largest Marine Corps bases in the world, covering more than 150,000 acres of land (including 22 miles of beachfront). Approximately 40,000 Marines and sailors are stationed at the base, along with their families.
