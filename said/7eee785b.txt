You can make a cheddar cheese sauce without any cheddar cheese by using other types of cheese. Here's a recipe for a sauce that uses Monterey Jack cheese, cream cheese, and sour cream:

Ingredients:

• 4 ounces monterey jack cheese, shredded
• 2 ounces cream cheese, softened
• 1/4 cup sour cream
• 2 tablespoons butter
• 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
• 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
• 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
• 1 cup milk
• Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. In a saucepan, melt the butter.
2. Add the flour, garlic powder, and onion powder. Stir until the mixture is smooth.
3. Slowly add the milk, stirring constantly.
4. Add the shredded cheese, cream cheese, and sour cream.
5. Heat the mixture on medium-low heat, stirring constantly, until the cheese is fully melted.
6. Add salt and pepper to taste.
7. Serve warm over your favorite dishes. Enjoy!
