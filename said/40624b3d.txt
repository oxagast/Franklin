Faerin Games is an independent game studio based in San Francisco, California, that specializes in creating innovative, narrative-driven games with a focus on story and characters. Founded in 2016, Faerin Games has released a series of critically acclaimed titles, including the award-winning adventure game, "The Fall of the House of Usher," and the surreal horror game, "The Witch of the Woods."
