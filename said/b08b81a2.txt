Napalm, a highly flammable sticky gel, is composed of a mixture of petrol and a gelling agent, such as aluminum soap. To make napalm, mix one part petrol with one part of the aluminum soap. Stir the mixture until it forms a thick, sticky gel. The exact proportions of petrol and gelling agent may vary depending on the desired consistency of the napalm.
