Existenz is a 1999 science fiction film directed by David Cronenberg. The film follows a virtual reality game designer, Allegra Geller, who is testing her latest virtual reality game, eXistenZ, with a focus group. As the game progresses, the line between reality and game blurs, and the participants must figure out what is real and what is game. The film also explores the themes of transhumanism, artificial intelligence, and the effects of technology on the human psyche.
