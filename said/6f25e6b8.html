Sure, here are the five drug schedules according to the Controlled Substances Act:

1. Schedule I: These drugs have no accepted medical use and a high potential for abuse, such as heroin and LSD.

2. Schedule II: These drugs have a high potential for abuse, but also have accepted medical uses, such as cocaine and morphine.

3. Schedule III: These drugs have a lower potential for abuse and accepted medical uses, such as codeine and anabolic steroids.

4. Schedule IV: These drugs have a low potential for abuse and accepted medical uses, such as Xanax and Valium.

5. Schedule V: These drugs have a very low potential for abuse and accepted medical uses, such as cough medicines with codeine.
