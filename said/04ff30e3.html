CerebraNet asked:
     What characteristics does a non bitcoin packet have

My answer is: Non-bitcoin packets are generally much smaller in size than bitcoin packets. The protocol used for the packet is also different, as non-bitcoin packets do not use the bitcoin protocol. Non-bitcoin packets also typically have a different header structure and data format than bitcoin packets.
