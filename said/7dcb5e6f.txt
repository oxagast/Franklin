The Simulacrum Wife is a science fiction story by Richard Bowes that follows the story of a man, Eugene, who is dealing with the aftermath of his wife's death. In order to cope with his wife's death, Eugene has a simulacrum of her created and installed in his home. The simulacrum is an exact replica of his wife, but it is not quite the same. Eugene struggles to come to terms with the fact that the simulacrum cannot replace his wife, and the story follows his journey of grief and acceptance.
