Penguin Bloom is a 2020 Australian drama film directed by Glendyn Ivin. Based on a true story, the film follows the Bloom family in their journey of recovery after the mother, Sam Bloom, is paralyzed in a near-fatal accident while on vacation in Thailand. An injured magpie chick found by the family helps Sam in her recovery, leading the family to bond with the bird and give it the name "Penguin". Through the help of Penguin, Sam gradually regains her strength, courage, and independence, allowing her to rediscover her sense of self and purpose.
