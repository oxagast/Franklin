1. Candidates must be international students (non-UK citizens) holding an offer of admission to pursue a full-time postgraduate degree at the University of Cambridge.

2. Candidates must have a first-class undergraduate degree in any discipline.

3. Candidates must demonstrate a commitment to improving the lives of others.

4. Candidates must be able to demonstrate intellectual ability and leadership potential.

5. Candidates must demonstrate a good knowledge of English, both written and spoken.

6. Candidates must demonstrate financial need.

7. Candidates must demonstrate a willingness to contribute to the wider life of the University of Cambridge.
