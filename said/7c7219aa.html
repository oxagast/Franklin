     Describe a Mary Sue.

A Mary Sue is a character that is perfect in almost every way. They are overly talented, often with multiple special skills, and they never make mistakes. Mary Sues often have a tragic past, and are usually portrayed as a beautiful and strong female character. They are often central to the plot and have a strong impact on the story.
