North Carolina is a state located in the southeastern United States. It is the 28th largest state by area and the 9th most populous state. It is bordered by Virginia to the north, South Carolina and Georgia to the south, and Tennessee to the west. The climate of North Carolina is generally mild, with the coastal areas receiving more precipitation than the western part of the state. North Carolina is home to many historical sites, outdoor recreation areas, and cultural attractions. It is also known for its diverse geography, which includes beaches, mountains, rivers, and lakes. Economic activities in the state include agriculture, manufacturing, finance, and technology.
