Biohacking is a term used to describe the practice of using technology and other methods to improve or enhance one's own body and mind. It can include things like implanting RFID or NFC chips into one's body, as well as using various supplements and devices to improve physical and cognitive performance. There are many ethical and legal implications to consider when it comes to biohacking, and it is a controversial topic among many communities.
