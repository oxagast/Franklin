1. Log into your web host or server and open the SSL Certificate Management page.

2. Locate the SSL certificate that needs to be renewed and click on the “Renew” button.

3. Enter the details of your new certificate, such as the domain name, company name, and validity period.

4. Generate a Certificate Signing Request (CSR) and submit it to the SSL vendor.

5. Once the certificate is approved, download the new certificate files and install them on your web server.

6. Test the new certificate to make sure it is working correctly.
