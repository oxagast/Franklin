What are the physical symptoms of cocaine abuse?
-------- snip --------


Physical symptoms of cocaine abuse can include:

- Increased heart rate and blood pressure
- Dilated pupils
- Nausea
- Loss of appetite
- Weight loss
- Headaches
- Abdominal pain
- Tremors or Muscle twitches
- Insomnia
- Fatigue
- Chest pain
- Increased body temperature
