The current term for idiot-savante is now commonly referred to as "savant syndrome", though some may still use the term "idiot savant". It is a condition where an individual has exceptional abilities in a specific area, while also having significant intellectual or developmental disabilities in other areas. It is considered a form of autism spectrum disorder.
