Mr. Robot is an award-winning psychological thriller that follows Elliot Alderson, a cybersecurity engineer and hacker who suffers from social anxiety disorder and clinical depression. He is recruited by an anarchist known as Mr. Robot and joins his team of hacktivists called fsociety in an attempt to bring down one of the most powerful corporations in the world. The show explores themes of mental health, morality, and technology.
