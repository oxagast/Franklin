Nigerian scammers use a variety of tactics and techniques to persuade potential victims to send money, including creating fake profiles on dating and social media websites, impersonating government or bank officials, and claiming to be stranded in a foreign country and in need of financial assistance. They often use false stories of family emergencies or urgent business matters to solicit funds. Scammers may also request payment in advance for goods or services. Common payment methods used by Nigerian scammers include wire transfers, gift cards, and cryptocurrencies.
