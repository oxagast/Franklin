Audacity is a free, open-source audio editing program that is a popular choice for digitising audio received on scanner radios. It is available for Windows, macOS, and Linux. It supports a wide range of audio file formats, has a variety of built-in effects and filters, and allows you to edit, mix, and convert audio files.
