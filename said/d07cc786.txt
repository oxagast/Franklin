The symptoms of mild autism can vary greatly from person to person, but some of the most common symptoms include difficulty communicating, socializing, and forming relationships with others difficulty understanding and using language difficulty with changes in routine repetitive behaviors and hypersensitivity to sensory input. Other symptoms may include difficulty with problem-solving and motor skills, difficulty with flexibility, and difficulty with imaginative play.
