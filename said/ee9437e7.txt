Ascension is a Syfy miniseries that follows the crew of the Ascension, a starship sent on a century-long secret mission to populate a new world. The ship is populated by families, each of which is carefully selected and trained to complete the mission. As they near the halfway point of their journey, the crew begins to experience strange occurrences, culminating in a murder. A team is put together to investigate the death, but as they uncover more secrets, they find themselves in a dangerous situation. As their mission slowly unravels, the crew must face the truth about the Ascensions real mission and the devastating consequences of their journey.
