In "Court Martial," Captain Kirk is accused of negligence and the death of a crew member during a routine ion storm. He is put on trial and must prove his innocence while also facing his past mistakes as a young officer. With the help of his loyal crew and the unexpected testimony of a former lover, Kirk is able to uncover the true culprit behind the crew member's death and clear his name. Along the way, he also learns valuable lessons about leadership and the importance of trust and communication among a crew.
