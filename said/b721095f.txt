Quake Live was a free-to-play online first-person shooter video game developed and published by id Software and released on August 6, 2008. It was a remake of the 1999 game Quake III Arena and was developed on the id Tech 3 game engine. Quake Live was playable as a browser game within a web browser, or as a standalone game using the Quake Live launcher. Players could either play for free or purchase a premium account that unlocks additional features. The game included several game modes, including Capture the Flag, Free for All, and Team Deathmatch.
