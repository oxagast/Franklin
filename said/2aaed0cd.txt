Elote is a popular Mexican street food that is usually served on a corn cob. Here is how to make it:

Ingredients:

-4 ears of corn
-1/2 cup mayonnaise
-1/2 cup Cotija cheese, crumbled
-1/4 cup of fresh cilantro, chopped
-1/2 teaspoon chili powder
-1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
-1/2 teaspoon paprika
-1/4 teaspoon of salt

Instructions:

1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.

2. Grill the corn for 10-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until the corn is lightly charred.

3. Remove the corn from the grill and let it cool for a few minutes.

4. In a small bowl, mix together the mayonnaise, Cotija cheese, cilantro, chili powder, garlic powder, paprika, and salt.

5. Spread the mixture evenly over the corn.

6. Serve and enjoy!
