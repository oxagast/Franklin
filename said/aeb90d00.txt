How do I configure easyminer to solo mine?
-------- snip --------


1. Download and install EasyMiner.
2. Go to the "Settings" menu and select the "Mining" tab.
3. Select "Solo Mining" from the Mining Mode drop-down menu.
4. Enter your wallet address in the Wallet Address field and click "Save".
5. Select the cryptocurrency you would like to mine from the list of available coins.
6. Enter the pool URL and port number in the Server field.
7. Enter your username and password in the appropriate fields.
8. Click "Save and Start" to begin solo mining.
