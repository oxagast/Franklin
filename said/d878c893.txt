It is headed by the Patriarch of Moscow and all Russia and is a self-governing body within the Russian Orthodox Church. The Russian Orthodox Church Outside of Russia is the state-owned church and is headed by the Patriarch of Moscow and all Russia. It was created in 1927 and is estimated to have 10 million members.
