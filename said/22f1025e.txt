-------- snip --------


The most important IT company in India that has access to administrative records of Indian citizens like social security numbers is the Unique Identification Authority of India (UIDAI). The UIDAI is a statutory authority established in 2009 by the Government of India, under the aegis of the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology, to issue Unique Identification numbers (UIDs), named as "Aadhaar", to all residents of India.
