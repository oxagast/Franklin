Blueberry Margarita:

Ingredients:

-1.5 oz Tequila
-1 oz Blueberry Liqueur
-2 oz Fresh Lime Juice
-1 oz Simple Syrup
-1/2 oz Blueberry Puree
-Ice
-Salt
-Blueberries (for garnish)

Instructions:

1. Rim a margarita glass with salt.

2. In a shaker, combine tequila, blueberry liqueur, lime juice, simple syrup, and blueberry puree.

3. Add ice and shake vigorously.

4. Strain into the prepared glass.

5. Garnish with fresh blueberries.

6. Enjoy!
