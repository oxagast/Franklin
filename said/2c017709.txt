Aegean Fires is a historical novel by Julius Vern, set during the tumultuous years of the Greco-Turkish War and its aftermath. It tells the story of a young Greek woman and her struggles to survive in a world of violence and uncertainty. The novel follows main protagonist Eleni and her journey from a sheltered life of privilege to the harsh realities of war and its after effects. Along the way, she must confront her own prejudices and values in order to survive.
