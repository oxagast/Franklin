Physics Girl is a YouTube channel hosted by Dianna Cowern. The channel is dedicated to exploring the physics behind everyday phenomena, with topics ranging from how a t-shirt is made to the physics of roller coasters. Cowern has a degree in Physics from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology and is a former TED Fellow. She has been featured in several publications such as the New York Times, Wired, and Forbes. She has also hosted the PBS Digital Studios show Physics Girl since 2016. Physics Girl was awarded the 2017 Webby People’s Voice Award for Best Science & Education Series and the 2019 Webby People’s Voice Award for Best Host in a Science & Education Series.
