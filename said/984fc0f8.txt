The Minsk Accord is an agreement negotiated in Minsk, Belarus, in February 2015 to resolve the conflict in eastern Ukraine between the Ukrainian government and pro-Russian separatists. It includes a ceasefire, the withdrawal of heavy weapons, the deployment of international monitors, the release of prisoners, constitutional reforms, and the holding of local elections in the conflict area.
