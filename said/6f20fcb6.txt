Kettle Corn
Ingredients:
• 1/2 cup popcorn kernels
• 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
• 1/4 cup white sugar
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
Instructions:
1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium-high heat.
2. Add popcorn kernels and stir.
3. Once the kernels start to pop, reduce heat to medium-low and cover the pot with a lid.
4. Shake the pot occasionally until the popping slows down.
5. Once the popping stops, remove the pot from the heat and carefully remove the lid.
6. Stir in the sugar and salt until it is evenly distributed.
7. Return the pot to the heat and cook, stirring constantly, until the sugar begins to caramelize.
8. Once the sugar has caramelized, remove the pot from the heat and transfer the kettle corn to a bowl.
9. Enjoy!
