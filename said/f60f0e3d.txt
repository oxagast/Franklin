Sure, here are some pros and cons of popular email clients:

Pros:

- Gmail: Large storage capacity, great user interface and easy integration with other Google services.

- Outlook: Easy to organize emails, provides a calendar and task manager.

- Apple Mail: Syncs well with other Apple devices.

Cons:

- Gmail: Can be difficult to find emails due to the large storage capacity.

- Outlook: Can be difficult to manage multiple accounts.

- Apple Mail: Can be slow to sync with other devices.
