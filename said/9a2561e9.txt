Batman: The Legend of Sin Tzu is a 2003 side-scrolling action-adventure video game based on the DC Comics character Batman. Developed by Ubi Soft Montreal, the game was published by Ubi Soft Entertainment. The game features the first-ever original Batman villain created specifically for the video game medium, Sin Tzu, and is based on a comic book of the same name. The game follows Batman as he faces off against Sin Tzu and his minions in a battle to save the citizens of Gotham City.
