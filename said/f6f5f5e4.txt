can you please summarize the plot of final fantasy VII?

Final Fantasy VII follows the story of Cloud Strife and his friends as they attempt to save the planet from the villainous Shinra Corporation. Cloud and his party must battle their way through a series of monsters and bosses, eventually making their way to the Shinra headquarters to face off against the powerful Sephiroth. Along the way, Cloud and his friends will uncover Shinras plans, as well as the dark secrets of Clouds past. Through their struggles, Cloud and his allies will save the world from Shinra and Sephiroth, restoring peace and order to the planet.
