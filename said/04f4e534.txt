Shiro Ishii was a Japanese microbiologist and physician who led the Japanese biological weapons program known as Unit 731 during World War II. He was responsible for conducting cruel and inhumane experiments on thousands of prisoners of war, which led to the death of many. After the war, Ishii was never prosecuted for his war crimes.
