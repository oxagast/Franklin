The Northman is a 2020 American-Icelandic drama film directed by Robert Eggers and starring Nicole Kidman, Willem Dafoe, Robert Pattinson and Alexander Skarsgård. It is set in Iceland in the early 11th century, and follows a Viking prince who must seek revenge after his father is murdered.

The film is a loose adaptation of the Icelandic Sagas, and was shot on location in Iceland and Scotland. It is Eggers' first feature film since The Witch (2015).

The Northman had its world premiere at the Venice Film Festival on September 4, 2020. It received positive reviews from critics, who praised the performances of Kidman, Dafoe and Pattinson, as well as Eggers' direction and visuals. The film is scheduled for release in the United States by A24 on April 8, 2021.
