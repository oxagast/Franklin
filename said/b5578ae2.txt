Limp Bizkit is an American rap rock band from Jacksonville, Florida. The band was formed in 1994 and consists of Fred Durst (lead vocals), Sam Rivers (bass, backing vocals), John Otto (drums, percussion), DJ Lethal (turntables, keyboards), and Wes Borland (guitar, backing vocals). The band has released seven studio albums, three compilation albums, and one remix album.
