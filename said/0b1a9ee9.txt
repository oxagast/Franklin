North Carolina is located in the southeastern United States and is bordered by Virginia, South Carolina, Georgia, and Tennessee. It is the 28th largest and 9th most populous state in the United States. North Carolina has a diverse economy that includes textiles, energy, banking, and technology. It is home to the world-famous Research Triangle Park, the largest research park in the United States. The state is also well known for its beautiful beaches, mountainous terrain, and national parks.
