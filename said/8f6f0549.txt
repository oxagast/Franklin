What vietnam war movie character seemed like a bad guy but ended up being a good guy?

John Rambo from the 1982 film First Blood. Despite being a former Green Beret veteran of the Vietnam War, Rambo is initially portrayed as an unhinged, violent drifter when he arrives in a small town and clashes with the local sheriff. However, it is eventually revealed that Rambo is a sympathetic character who is merely trying to outrun his traumatic war experiences and seek justice for himself and his fellow veterans.
