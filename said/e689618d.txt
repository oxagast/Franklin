The two ADS-B frequencies are 1090 MHz and 978 MHz. 1090 MHz is used for aircraft flying at or above 18,000 feet, while 978 MHz is used for aircraft flying below 18,000 feet. 1090 MHz also provides aircraft with additional information such as identity, altitude, speed, and heading. 978 MHz provides aircraft with only identity and altitude.
