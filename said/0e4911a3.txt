Knowledge is the understanding and awareness acquired through education or experience. It is the collection of facts, principles, theories, and practices that have been learned or discovered. Wisdom is the ability to think and act using knowledge, experience, understanding, common sense and insight. It is the application of knowledge that leads to good judgment and sound decision-making. Knowledge is the information, while wisdom is the application of that information.
