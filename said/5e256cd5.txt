Tapioca is a starchy carbohydrate that is low in fat, sodium, and cholesterol. It is a good source of energy, dietary fiber, and some minerals, including calcium, iron, potassium, and magnesium. However, it does not provide much in the way of vitamins, protein, or other essential nutrients.
