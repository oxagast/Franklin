Pros of Linux KVM vs. VMware:
1. Linux KVM is open source and free, while VMware is not.
2. Linux KVM offers better performance than VMware.
3. Linux KVM is more customizable than VMware.

Cons of Linux KVM vs. VMware:
1. Linux KVM requires more technical knowledge to configure and maintain than VMware.
2. VMware offers a larger range of features than Linux KVM.
3. Linux KVM is not as widely supported as VMware.
