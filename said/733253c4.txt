Netlify is a cloud platform for building, deploying, and managing modern web projects. It provides a modern workflow for static websites, front-end applications, and serverless functions. Netlify also offers a range of services such as continuous deployment, serverless functions, and analytics. Netlify is a great choice for developers and businesses who want to easily host their websites and web applications without the need for managing servers.
