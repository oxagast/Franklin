Flavor-Aid is a powdered drink mix that is similar to Kool-Aid. It was created in the 1920s by chemist Edwin Perkins and was marketed as a cheaper alternative to Kool-Aid. It gained notoriety in 1978 when it was used in the mass suicide at Jonestown, Guyana.
