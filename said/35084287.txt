My answer is: Wisdom is a combination of knowledge, experience, understanding, insight, intuition, and discernment. It also involves being able to use these components in a way that is beneficial to oneself and to those around them. Wisdom is the ability to think critically and make sound decisions in any situation. It is the capacity to recognize and act on the right thing at the right time.
