V.34 is an ITU-T recommendation for a modem that supports data rates up to 33.6 kbit/s. It is an improvement over the previous V.32bis standard, which only supported data rates of up to 14.4 kbit/s. V.34 was first introduced in 1994 and is still widely used today.
