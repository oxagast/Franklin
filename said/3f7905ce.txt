Vietnamese-Swedish Paella 

Ingredients:

-1 cup long-grain rice
-1 teaspoon olive oil
-1 onion, chopped
-2 cloves garlic, minced
-1 red bell pepper, sliced
-1/2 cup cooked shrimp
-1/2 cup cooked chicken, diced
-1/2 cup cooked pork, diced
-1/2 cup cooked Vietnamese sausage, diced
-1/2 cup cooked Swedish meatballs, diced
-1 cup canned diced tomatoes
-2 cups chicken broth
-1 teaspoon cumin
-1/2 teaspoon paprika
-1/4 teaspoon turmeric
-1/4 teaspoon saffron
-1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
-1/4 cup frozen peas
-1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
-Lemon wedges, for serving

Instructions:

1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the onions and garlic, and cook for about 3 minutes, until the onions are softened.

2. Add the bell pepper, shrimp, chicken, pork, sausage, and meatballs and cook for another 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.

3. Stir in the rice, tomatoes, chicken broth, cumin, paprika, turmeric, saffron, and ground black pepper. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low. Cover, and simmer for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.

4. Add the peas and cook for an additional 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the rice is tender.

5. Remove from heat and stir in the parsley. Serve with lemon wedges. Enjoy!
