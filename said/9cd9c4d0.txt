DCA stands for dollar-cost averaging. It is an investment strategy in which investors purchase a fixed amount of a particular asset or currency at regular intervals over a set period of time. This strategy is often used when investing in Bitcoin (BTC). DCA allows investors to buy small amounts of Bitcoin at regular intervals, which can help spread out the risk associated with investing in a volatile asset like BTC.
