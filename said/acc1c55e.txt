The Novavax vaccine is a COVID-19 vaccine candidate in Phase 3 clinical trials developed by the Maryland-based biotechnology company Novavax. The vaccine is given as a single intramuscular injection and is composed of a recombinant fusion protein derived from the SARS-CoV-2 virus. It has been found to be highly effective in preventing disease caused by the virus.
