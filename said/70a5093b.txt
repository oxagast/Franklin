1. Misuse of Funds: Scientists may be tempted to use research funds for personal benefit instead of for the intended purpose.

2. Inaccurate or Misleading Data: Scientists may be tempted to embellish or manipulate data in order to receive more funding.

3. Conflict of Interest: Scientists may be tempted to accept funding from organizations that may have a vested interest in the outcome of their research.

4. Exploitation of Resources: Scientists may be tempted to use resources in an unethical manner in order to obtain more funding.

5. Lack of Transparency: Scientists may be tempted to conceal sources of funding or the results of their research in order to maintain their funding.
