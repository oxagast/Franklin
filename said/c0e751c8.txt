Scalped is a comic book series that follows the story of Dashiell Bad Horse, a Native American who returns to his reservation after a long absence and takes a job as a federal agent. After infiltrating the local criminal underworld, Dashiell discovers a tangled web of corruption and exploitation involving various factions, including the tribal council, the police, local businesses, and the FBI. As Dashiell unravels the mystery, he must face his own past as well as the violence and prejudice of the reservation. Scalped is a gritty, action-packed story that delves into the complexities of life on a Native American reservation.
