A romance scam is a type of fraud that involves an individual pretending to be in an online romantic relationship with another person in order to obtain money or other items of value. The scammer may pretend to be an attractive love interest and use various tactics to gain the trust of their victim. They may also send messages claiming to be from a third party in order to gain access to personal information or financial data.
