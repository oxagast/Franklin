Ayn Rand is a philosopher because she developed a philosophical system called Objectivism, which is based on her belief in the moral supremacy of the individual and the rejection of collectivism. Her philosophical works have been widely influential, and her ideas have been cited by many later thinkers, including those from the political right and left.
