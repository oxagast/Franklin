Nusret Gökçe, better known as Salt Bae, is a Turkish chef, restaurateur, and social media personality. He is known for his skill in preparing and seasoning meat, as well as for his creative plating of dishes, which often includes the dramatic sprinkling of salt. He achieved international fame in 2017 after a video of him sprinkling salt in a theatrical manner went viral. He owns a chain of steak restaurants called Nusr-Et, which has branches in several countries.
