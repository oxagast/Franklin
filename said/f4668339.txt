A phor is a figure of speech that involves a comparison between two things without using the words “like” or “as.” Examples of a phor include: 

1. "All the world's a stage" (William Shakespeare)
2. "Life is a journey"
3. "Love is a battlefield"
4. "Time is money"
5. "A rolling stone gathers no moss"
