Indian philosophy focuses more on the spiritual, metaphysical and religious aspects of life, while Western philosophy focuses more on the physical and material aspects of life. Indian philosophy tends to be more holistic and contemplative, while Western philosophy tends to be more analytical and objectively oriented. Indian philosophy is more concerned with the inner life and spiritual evolution of the individual, while Western philosophy is more concerned with the physical and material world around us.
