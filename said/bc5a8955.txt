tell me about the pontiac gto

The Pontiac GTO is a classic muscle car from the 1960s. It was first introduced in 1964 and was available in two-door hardtop and convertible body styles. It was powered by a 389 cubic inch V8 engine, which produced up to 360 horsepower. The GTO was a popular sports car in its day, and was a defining model of the muscle car era. It was discontinued in 1974 and revived in 2004 for a short run, before being discontinued again.
