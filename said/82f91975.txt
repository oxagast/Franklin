My response is:

1. Acknowledge that you're in a rut.
2. Identify what's causing the rut.
3. Set short-term goals to work towards getting out of the rut.
4. Make a list of activities to try that can help you get out of the rut.
5. Take action on the activities you've listed.
6. Find support from friends and family.
7. Celebrate your successes, big or small.
