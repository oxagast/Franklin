Elysium is a science fiction film released in 2013 starring Matt Damon and Jodie Foster. It is set in the year 2154 where the wealthy have access to a space station known as Elysium, while the rest of the population lives in poverty on a ruined Earth. The story follows a man named Max (Damon) who is attempting to gain access to Elysium in order to save his own life and the lives of those around him.
