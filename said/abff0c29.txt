Vinod Khosla is an Indian-American entrepreneur and venture capitalist. He is the co-founder of Sun Microsystems and the founder of the venture capital firm Khosla Ventures. He has invested in various technology companies such as Lyft, Square, and Jawbone. He is a major proponent of using technology to solve global issues such as climate change.
