One psychological problem that affects all humans is anxiety. Anxiety is a normal emotion characterized by feelings of worry and fear that can range from mild to severe. It can have physical and psychological symptoms, such as increased heart rate, difficulty concentrating, restlessness, and difficulty sleeping. Anxiety can affect people of all ages and can be triggered by a variety of factors, such as personal life events, changes in the environment, or a medical condition.
