Trotskyists
-------- snip --------


Trotskyists are followers of Leon Trotsky, a Soviet revolutionary, Marxist theorist, and one of the original leaders of the Russian Revolution. Trotskyists believe in the need for an international socialist revolution and the establishment of a world socialist state. They are committed to the idea of permanent revolution, which posits that socialism must be constantly renewed and reinvented in order to survive. Trotskyists advocate for democratic control of the economy and of the state.
