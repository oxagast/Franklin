What are some examples of opioid potentiators? 
-------- snip --------


Examples of opioid potentiators include: 

- Benzodiazepines (e.g., Diazepam, Lorazepam)

- Anticonvulsants (e.g., Gabapentin, Pregabalin)

- Antidepressants (e.g., Tricyclic Antidepressants, Selective Serotonin Reuptake Inhibitors)

- Beta blockers (e.g., Propranolol)

- Anti-inflammatories (e.g., Celecoxib, Ibuprofen)

- Alpha2-adrenergic agonists (e.g., Clonidine)

- N-methyl-d-aspartate (NMDA) receptor antagonists (e.g., Ketamine, Dextromethorphan)

- Mu-opioid receptor agonists (e.g., Buprenorphine, Butorphanol)
