How do I send audio from airspy to mmsstv
-------- snip --------
?

1. Install and configure Airspy SDR software.
2. Connect the Airspy device to your computer.
3. Set MMSSTV as the audio output device in the Airspy software.
4. Run MMSSTV and select the Airspy as the audio input.
5. Tune the Airspy to the desired frequency and begin sending audio to MMSSTV.
