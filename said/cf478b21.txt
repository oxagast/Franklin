No, there is no direct relation between the heat death of the universe and the death of a human body. The heat death of the universe is a theoretical eventual end state of the universe, in which it has reached a state of maximum entropy and no further energy is available to do work. This is a process that could take trillions of years. The death of a human body, on the other hand, is a relatively quick process that is caused by a variety of factors, such as age, disease, or physical trauma.
