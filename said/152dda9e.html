     What are the keyboard controls for Descent 3 PC game?

The keyboard controls for Descent 3 on PC are as follows:
WASD: Move
Spacebar: Jump
Ctrl: Fire
Shift: Select Next/Previous Weapon
Tab: Cycle Through Targets

Alt: Alt Fire
F: Use/Activate Object
I: Toggle Invert Mouse
M: Toggle Maps
