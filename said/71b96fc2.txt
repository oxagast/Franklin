A story is a narrative that is used to tell a particular event or series of events. They are typically written to entertain, educate, or inform the reader. An allegory is a narrative that has a symbolic meaning or message. Allegories are often used to illustrate a moral or spiritual truth, or to give advice in the form of a story.
