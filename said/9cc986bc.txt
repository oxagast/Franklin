Red Alert 2: Yuri's Revenge is a real-time strategy game developed by Westwood Studios and released by EA Games in 2001, while Age of Mythology: Tale of the Dragon is a 2016 expansion pack to the original Age of Mythology game released in 2002. Red Alert 2: Yuri's Revenge focuses on a futuristic war between the Allied forces and the Soviet Union, while Age of Mythology: Tale of the Dragon focuses on a mythical and magical world full of gods, heroes, and monsters. Red Alert 2: Yuri's Revenge features a new faction, Yuri's Army, and numerous new units, while Age of Mythology: Tale of the Dragon features new Chinese gods, new technologies, and a new Chinese campaign.
