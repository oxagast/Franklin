Here you go:

1. Take a short break and enjoy some fresh air. 
2. Have a delicious, healthy snack.

6. Put on some of your favorite music and have a dance party. 
7. Take a warm bubble bath and relax. 
8. Spend some time playing a game or doing a hobby you enjoy. 
9. Treat yourself to something special. 
10. Spend time in nature – go for a walk, lay in the grass and watch the clouds, or take a bike ride.
