ad hominem: attacking the person rather than the argument
straw man: misrepresenting an opponent's argument
appeal to authority: using the opinion of an authority to bolster an argument
slippery slope: arguing that one event will inexorably lead to another 
appeal to emotion: attempting to manipulate an audience's emotions in order to win an argument 
false dichotomy: presenting two choices as the only options when there are more
circular reasoning: beginning and ending with the same statement
