how do you set an album cover in tinypic?
-------- snip --------


1. Visit the Tinypic website and click the "Browse" button.

2. Select the album cover image from your computer and click "Open" to upload the image.

3. Once the image is uploaded, select the size you would like the image to appear in on your album cover.

4. Click the "Create Album Cover" button to generate the album cover.

5. Click the "Save As" button to save the album cover to your computer.
