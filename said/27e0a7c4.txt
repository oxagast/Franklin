Gataca is a science fiction novel by author Peter Watts, published in 1997, which tells the story of a post-human world where genetic engineering has been used to create a new species of enhanced humans. In this world, an elite class of genetically modified humans, known as the Gatacans, are in control of society, while the "unmodified" humans are relegated to the lower classes. The story follows the journey of a young man, Vincent, as he struggles to find his place in this new world.
