     Is DMR a form of modulation like AM or FM

DMR is not a form of modulation like AM or FM. It is a digital radio communication system that is used for two-way digital voice communication. It does not offer the same frequency modulation capabilities as AM or FM, but it does offer some protection against eavesdropping.
