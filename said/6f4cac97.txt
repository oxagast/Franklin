They have an upright stance, with a long neck and a bill that curves slightly downward. French ducks are good layers, and they lay white, pinkish-gray, or blue-green eggs. They are also good foragers, and they are known for their hardiness and adaptability. French ducks are considered one of the best dual-purpose ducks, as they make good meats birds as well as egg layers.
