1. Start by making the filling. In a large bowl, mix together 1 pound of ground beef, 1 cup of cooked white rice, 1/2 cup of finely chopped onion, 1/4 cup of finely chopped fresh parsley, 1 teaspoon of garlic powder, 1 teaspoon of salt, 1/2 teaspoon of freshly ground black pepper and 2 tablespoons of olive oil. 

2. Heat a large skillet over medium heat and add 2 tablespoons of olive oil. Add the ground beef mixture to the skillet and cook until the beef is cooked through, about 8 minutes. 

3. While the beef is cooking, make the cabbage rolls. Remove the core from a head of cabbage and place in a large pot of boiling water. Boil the cabbage until it is tender, about 20 minutes. Carefully remove the leaves from the boiling water and allow them to cool.

4. Place 1-2 tablespoons of the beef filling in the center of a cabbage leaf
