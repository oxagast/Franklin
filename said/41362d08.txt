Duck Breasts with Orange Sauce

Ingredients:

• 4 boneless duck breasts

• Salt and pepper, to taste

• 2 tablespoons butter

• 2 tablespoons olive oil

• 1/4 cup orange juice

• 2 tablespoons honey

• 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard

• 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

Instructions:

1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).

2. Season the duck breasts with salt and pepper.

3. Heat the butter and olive oil in a large oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat.

4. Add the duck breasts to the skillet and cook for 3-4 minutes per side until golden brown.

5. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for 10-12 minutes.

6. Meanwhile, in a small bowl, whisk together the orange juice, honey, and Dijon mustard until combined.

7. Remove the skillet from the oven and transfer the duck breasts to a plate.

8. Pour the orange sauce into the skillet and cook over medium-high heat for 2-3 minutes until the sauce is reduced and slightly thickened.

9. Return the duck breasts to the skillet and spoon the sauce over the top.

10. Sprinkle with fresh parsley and serve. Enjoy!
