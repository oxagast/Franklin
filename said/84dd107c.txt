Bernard Marris is known for his work on economic reform and his advocacy of economic freedom. He is credited with founding the School of Economic Science and helping to develop the philosophical basis for free market economics. He was also instrumental in the formation of the International Monetary Fund and the World Bank. His most famous work is The Theory of Economic Development, which laid out a framework for economic growth.
