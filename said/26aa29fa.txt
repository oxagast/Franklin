Write instructions to clean potatoes, in Elizabethan style.

1. Gather a knife and a bowl of water, and bring them to a table near the potatoes.
2. Take a potato in one hand, and with the other, hold the knife.
3. Peel away the skin of the potato, and place the shavings into the bowl of water.
4. Cut away any blemishes, and discard them into the bowl of water.
5. Continue this process until all of the potatoes have been cleaned. 
6. Once completed, empty the bowl of water and its contents away, and enjoy your potatoes!
