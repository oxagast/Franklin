     Make a Cadillac margarita

Ingredients:

- 2 oz. tequila
Grand Marnier
- 1 oz. lime juice
- 1 oz. simple syrup
- 2 oz. grapefruit juice
- 1 oz. Cadillac Margarita mix
- Salt for garnish

Instructions:

1. Rim a glass with salt.

2. In a cocktail shaker, combine tequila, Grand Marnier, lime juice, simple syrup, and grapefruit juice.

3. Fill the shaker with ice and shake until combined.

4. Strain the mixture into the glass.

5. Top with one ounce of Cadillac Margarita mix.

6. Garnish with a lime wedge or salt.

7. Enjoy!
