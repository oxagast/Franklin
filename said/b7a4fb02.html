oxagast asked:
     how do you make unicode and utf-8 characters show up properly in html?

To make Unicode and UTF-8 characters show up properly in HTML, you must add a meta tag to the head section of the HTML document with the UTF-8 character set. The meta tag should look like this:

<meta charset="utf-8">
