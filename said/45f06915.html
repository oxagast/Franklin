     Name an important historical short story

.

An important historical short story is "The Legend of Sleepy Hollow" by Washington Irving. It's a classic tale of the supernatural set in a tranquil village in colonial America. It is often cited as one of the earliest examples of American fiction and is one of the earliest horror stories.
