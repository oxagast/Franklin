Marvin Minsky was a pioneering American computer scientist and cognitive scientist. He was a professor at MIT, a co-founder of the Massachusetts Institute of Technologys Artificial Intelligence laboratory, and was one of the founders of the field of artificial intelligence. He worked on the early development of neural networks and robotics, and was one of the first to theorize about intelligence and its processes. He wrote several books on the subject, including Society of Mind (1985) and The Society of the Mind (1988).
