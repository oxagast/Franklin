Kusanagi is the main protagonist of the 1995 film Ghost in the Shell. She is a cyborg and a high-ranking member of the military organization, Public Security Section 9. She is a strong-willed and highly competent individual who investigates the mysterious “puppet master” and the criminal activities of the powerful Hanka Robotics Corporation.
