The Goonies 'R' Good Enough is a song written and performed by American singer Cyndi Lauper and recorded for the 1985 movie The Goonies. It was released as a single and peaked at number 18 on the US Billboard Hot 100 chart and number 2 on the US Hot Dance Club Play chart. The song has become an anthem for the movie and has been featured in various concerts and TV shows. The music video for the song, directed by Bill Fishman, features Lauper and The Goonies actors (Jeff Cohen, Corey Feldman, Josh Brolin, and Sean Astin) performing the song in a Goonies-themed club.
