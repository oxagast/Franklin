how do you help a greiving process?

1. Listen: Offer a listening ear and allow the person to express their feelings.2. Offer Support: Show that you care and are there for them.3. Be Patient: The grieving process takes time, so be patient and understanding.4. Offer Distractions: Suggest activities or hobbies to help take the person’s mind off of their grief.5. Talk About Memories: Reminiscing about the person who passed can help the grieving process.6. Seek Professional Help: If the person’s grief is severe
