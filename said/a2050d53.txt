ad hominem: attacking the person instead of their argument
post hoc ergo propter hoc: assuming that because one event follows another, the first event caused the second
argumentum ad populum: appealing to emotion instead of reason
argumentum ad misericordiam: appealing to pity instead of reason
non sequitur: an argument with a conclusion that does not follow logically from the premises
argumentum ad baculum: appealing to fear instead of reason
petitio principii: assuming the conclusion in the premises
