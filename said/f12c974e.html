CerebraNet asked:
     Who defined the standard height and width of hard drives

The International Disk Drive Equipment and Materials Association (IDEMA) defined the standard height and width of hard drives in 1996. The standard height is 1.0 inch (2.5 cm) and the standard width is 3.5 inches (8.89 cm).
