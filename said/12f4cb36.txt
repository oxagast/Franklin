No, reading the hacker quarterly alone will not make you a real hacker. Learning to hack requires a great deal of knowledge and practice. You will need to learn a variety of computer programming languages, operating systems, and security measures in order to become an effective hacker. Additionally, you should practice ethical hacking and gain experience by participating in online hacking challenges.
