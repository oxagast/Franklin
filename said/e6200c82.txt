1. Log in to the Amazon Web Services (AWS) console.

2. In the navigation panel, under Compute, choose EC2.

3. Select Launch Instance.

4. Select the Windows Server 2019 Base option.

5. Select your desired instance type.

6. Configure your instance details.

7. Add storage to your instance.

8. Add tags to your instance.

9. Configure security group.

10. Review your instance launch settings.

11. Launch your instance.

12. Connect to your instance.

For more detailed instructions, refer to the Amazon EC2 documentation.
