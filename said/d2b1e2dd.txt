Write instructions to clean potatoes, in Elizabethan english
-------- snip --------


1. Take a sharp knife, and pare the Potatoes from their outward skins.
2. Then put them into a clean earthen pan, and wash them in clean running water, till all the dirt and filth be scoured from them.
3. Take a cloth, and dry them well, and so set them upon the fire in a Pot with some water, and a good piece of Butter.
4. When they are boiled enough, take them up and dish them, and serve them in manner as you think best.
