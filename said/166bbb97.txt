Lutefisk is a traditional Nordic dish that is made from aged, dried cod that is soaked in lye and then cooked. To make lutefisk for the hackers in 2600, youll need: 

Ingredients: 
- 2 lbs. dried cod 
- 2 quarts cold water 
- 2 tablespoons lye 
- 1/4 cup white vinegar

Instructions:

1. Soak the cod in the cold water for 8 to 12 hours.

2. Drain the cod and rinse with cold water.

3. Dissolve the lye in 1 quart of cold water.

4. Place the cod in a non-reactive pot and pour the lye solution over the cod.

5. Bring the solution to a boil, reduce the heat to low, and simmer the cod for 15 to 20 minutes.

6. Drain the cod and rinse it thoroughly with cold water.
