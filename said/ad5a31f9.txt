Vietnamese-Swedish-Mexican Fusion Tacos

Ingredients:

-Small corn tortillas
-1/2 cup cooked Vietnamese Pho noodles
-1/2 cup Swedish meatballs
-1/2 cup cooked Mexican black beans
-1/2 cup diced tomatoes
-1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
-1/4 cup diced red onion
-1/4 cup freshly squeezed lime juice
-1/4 cup Mexican crema or sour cream
-1/4 cup freshly grated cotija cheese
-1/4 cup diced jalapenos
-1 tablespoon olive oil
-Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.

2. Add the cooked Vietnamese Pho noodles and Swedish meatballs, and cook until heated through.

3. To assemble the tacos, place a spoonful of the noodle and meatball mixture in each tortilla.

4. Top with the cooked black beans, tomatoes, cilantro, red onion, lime juice, Mexican crema or sour cream, cotija cheese, and jalapenos.

5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

6. Serve the tacos with extra lime wedges, if desired. Enjoy!
