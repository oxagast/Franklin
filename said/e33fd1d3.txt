can you please summarize the plot of final fantasy XII?

Final Fantasy XII follows the story of Vaan, a young man who dreams of becoming a sky pirate, and his unlikely allies as they fight against the sinister Empire to reclaim the land of Ivalice. Along the way, they discover a powerful artifact known as the "Dawn Shard", which could be their key to victory. With the help of the magical and mysterious Espers, they set out to restore peace and freedom to Ivalice. Along the way, they battle monsters, explore dungeons, and find new allies to join their cause.
