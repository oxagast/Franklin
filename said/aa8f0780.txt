Yes, both the Moderna and Pfizer-BioNTech vaccines are safe and effective. Clinical trials for both vaccines showed that they were about 95% effective in preventing COVID-19 infections. There have been some reports of adverse reactions, but these are very rare and typically mild. Both vaccines have been approved for emergency use by the US Food and Drug Administration and the European Medicines Agency.
