Vietnamese-Swedish-Spanish Rice Bowl:

Ingredients:

-1 cup long grain white rice

-1/2 cup jasmine rice

-1/2 cup chopped carrots

-1/2 cup sliced Spanish chorizo

-1/2 cup shredded Vietnamese pork (or chicken)

-1/4 cup Swedish pickled cucumbers

-1/4 cup finely chopped Spanish onion

-1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro

-2 tablespoons fish sauce

-2 tablespoons lime juice

-Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. Cook the long grain white rice and jasmine rice in separate pots according to package instructions.

2. Heat a large skillet over medium heat and add the carrots, Spanish chorizo, and Vietnamese pork. Cook until the pork is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.

3. Add the Swedish pickled cucumbers, Spanish onion, and cilantro to the skillet and stir to combine.

4. Add the cooked rice to the skillet and stir to combine.

5. Add the fish sauce and lime juice to the mixture and stir to combine.

6. Taste and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper.

7. Serve the rice bowl warm and enjoy!
