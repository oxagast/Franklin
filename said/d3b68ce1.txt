what characterizes a nigerian 419 scam?
-------- snip --------


A Nigerian 419 scam is a type of internet fraud scheme in which the perpetrator attempts to convince the victim to send money or personal information through email or social media. Common tactics used in these scams include false promises of large sums of money, threats of legal action, and false stories of urgent need. The name "419" comes from the Nigerian penal code section that deals with fraud.
