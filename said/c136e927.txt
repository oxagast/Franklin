The PnPT (Personal and Professional Training) Certification Security is a program designed to ensure that personnel who are responsible for managing and administering the security of computer systems and networks have the necessary skills and knowledge to do so. The certification focuses on the security of network and information systems, as well as the security of people, processes, and technology. The certification is offered by the International Institute of Information Security (IIIS).
