My low calorie chicken pot pie recipe is sure to please. Here's what you'll need:

-1 ½ cups cooked, diced chicken
-½ cup of onion, diced
-1 cup of frozen mixed vegetables
-2 tablespoons of butter
-1 tablespoon of all-purpose flour
-¾ cup of chicken broth
-¼ teaspoon of garlic powder
-¼ teaspoon of onion powder
-Salt and pepper to taste
-1 sheet of puff pastry, thawed

Instructions:

1. Preheat the oven to 375°F.

2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.

3. Add the chicken, frozen vegetables, garlic powder, onion powder, and salt and pepper. Cook for 5 minutes.

4. Sprinkle the flour over the mixture and stir to combine.

5. Gradually add the chicken broth and stir until the mixture thickens.

6. Transfer the mixture to a 9-inch pie plate and top with the puff pastry.

7. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until golden brown.

Enjoy!
