how to spot a nymphomaniac
-------- snip --------


It is not possible to spot a nymphomaniac based on physical characteristics or behavior. Nymphomania is a psychological disorder, which means that it can only be diagnosed by a qualified mental health professional. Symptoms of nymphomania include an unusually strong or frequent sex drive and difficulty controlling sexual impulses. Other signs include promiscuity, an obsession with sex and frequent sexual thoughts.
