Adventist Health Preregistration is a service offered by Adventist Health hospitals and medical care facilities that allows patients to preregister for their upcoming appointments online. This service simplifies the registration process and allows patients to fill out all of the necessary paperwork from the comfort of their own home. Preregistration can help reduce wait times and ensure that patients get the care they need in a timely manner.
