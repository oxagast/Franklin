Sure! Here's a simple, three-ingredient chocolate chip cookie recipe: 
1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. Combine 1 cup (115 g) of softened butter, 1/2 cup (100 g) of white sugar, and 1/2 cup (100 g) of brown sugar in a bowl. 
3. Mix in 1 teaspoon (5 ml) of vanilla extract, 2 cups (260 g) of all-purpose flour, 1 teaspoon (5 g) of baking soda, 1 teaspoon (5 g) of salt, and 1 cup (175 g) of semi-sweet chocolate chips.
4. Scoop spoonfuls of dough onto an ungreased baking sheet.
5. Bake for 8-10 minutes. Enjoy!
