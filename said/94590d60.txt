2-1/2 cups beef broth

1/4 cup dry red wine

1/4 cup tomato sauce

1/4 teaspoon dried thyme leaves

1/4 teaspoon garlic powder

1/4 teaspoon onion powder

1/4 teaspoon sugar

1/4 teaspoon black pepper

1/8 teaspoon celery salt

1 bay leaf

1/4 cup all-purpose flour

1/4 cup cold water

Instructions

In a large pot or Dutch oven, combine the beef broth, wine, tomato sauce, thyme, garlic powder, onion powder, sugar, black pepper, celery salt, and bay leaf. Bring to a boil over high heat.

Reduce the heat to low and carefully add the meatballs to the pot. Simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the meatballs are cooked through.
