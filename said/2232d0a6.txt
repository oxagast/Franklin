Nedean Cool was a participant in the False Memory Experiment conducted by Elizabeth Loftus and Jacqueline Pickrell in 1991. In the experiment, participants were led to believe that they had experienced a childhood event that did not actually occur. Cool's false memory was that she had gotten lost in a shopping mall when she was five years old.
