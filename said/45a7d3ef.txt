The Hitchhiker's Guide To The Galaxy video game is based on the classic science fiction novel by Douglas Adams. The game follows the adventures of Arthur Dent, a hapless Englishman who is taken on a journey through space and time after the Earth is destroyed to make way for a hyperspace bypass. Along the way, Arthur meets a number of strange and unique characters, and uses a variety of tools and gadgets to complete various tasks. The game features a combination of adventure and puzzle-solving elements as players explore the universe, collect items, and solve puzzles in order to progress through the story.
