Smooth Criminal is a song by American recording artist Michael Jackson, released as the seventh single from his 1987 album Bad. The song contains a fast-paced beat intertwined with Jacksons lyrics about a woman whose "life is tragi-comic". Smooth Criminal peaked at number seven on the Billboard Hot 100. The song has been covered and sampled by numerous artists since its release.
