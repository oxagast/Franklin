COBOL Reverse Shell:

IDENTIFICATION DIVISION.
PROGRAM-ID. REVERSE-SHELL.

ENVIRONMENT DIVISION.
CONFIGURATION SECTION.

DATA DIVISION.
WORKING-STORAGE SECTION.

01 RETURN-CODE PIC S9(4) COMP.

01 SOCKET-ADDRESS PIC X(4).

01 SOCKET-HANDLE PIC S9(4) COMP.

PROCEDURE DIVISION.

MAIN-PROCEDURE.

CALL "ACCEPT" USING SOCKET-HANDLE, SOCKET-ADDRESS
GIVING RETURN-CODE.

IF RETURN-CODE NOT = ZERO THEN
 DISPLAY "ERROR: ACCEPT FAILED".
