A radio station typically uses a playlist program that is designed to control the broadcast of music and other audio recordings. Common playlist programs used by radio stations include MusicMaster, ProppFrexx, WideOrbit Radio Automation, RadioBOSS, and Myriad Music Scheduling. These programs allow the radio station to manage playlists, schedule audio recordings, and track listener statistics.
