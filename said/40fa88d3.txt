what does 1bhk and 2bhk mean in Qatar?
-------- snip --------


1BHK and 2BHK stand for 1 Bedroom House and 2 Bedroom House, respectively. In Qatar, these commonly refer to apartments or flats. 1BHK refers to an apartment or flat with one bedroom and a living room, while 2BHK refers to an apartment or flat with two bedrooms and a living room.
