Summarize, how does lowering a daily dosage of klonopin cause symptoms of vertigo?
-------- snip --------


Lowering a daily dosage of Klonopin can cause symptoms of vertigo due to changes in the brain chemistry caused by the decreased medication. As the body adjusts to the lower dose, the balance system in the brain becomes confused, resulting in vertigo symptoms such as dizziness, lightheadedness, and a feeling of spinning.
